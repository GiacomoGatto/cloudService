Pre-Launch Phase

Our project is now developing into a platform. Our mission was accepted and entered the operative phase. Different teams in different countries are gathering materials and information to fashion a great user-experience in terms of design, IT and aerospace knowledge.

The platform will take the shape of a community interacting with tools and 'serious games', users are going to experience into living locations and involving atmosphere. There will be a common ground to interact with the tools. We want it to be a big construction site for ideas, assembling fields of knowledge, or connecting peripheral pieces of concepts, creating new possible ways to progress.

Because of the approach that drives our quest for learning, we will engage users with a perceptive immersion into logical thinking, graphics and sounds. We will tend to make them feel every virtual 'location' inside the platform as a audio-video installation surrounding a learning environment.

We want to describe data and space-related infotech through flow charts and graphs. To let users capture consistently patterns and directions in different know-hows.

We are designing some multiplayer action, to guide classes of users into proactive challenges to improve group members coordination and collaboration.

We plan massive use of Learn-By-Playing features in the platform, from a basic ‘starter kit’ with Newtonian physics and basic about payloads, to new gorgeous features and news for expert users (data-handling and data-visualization from real missions).

Stay tuned to know when our crowd-funding campaign will start!
